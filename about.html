Who We Are

International Journal of Research in Educational Studies (IJRES) is a peer-reviewed, open-access journal dedicated to advancing knowledge and innovation in the field of education.

We provide a global platform for researchers, scholars, and educators to publish high-quality research that contributes to academic excellence and educational development.

IJRES promotes ethical research practices, rigorous review standards, and timely publication, supporting the dissemination of impactful studies that shape the future of education worldwide.
